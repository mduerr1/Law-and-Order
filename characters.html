Top 5 Characters with link to Actors' Wikipedias

  1. Assistant District Attorney Jack McCoy

    Beginning his Law and Order tenure in season 5, ADA Jack McCoy prosecuted criminals for more than a decade. Folliwing his tenure as an ADA, Jack McCoy became the District Attorney, replacing his predecessor Arthur Branch. Known for his passion for his job and dedication to the law, Jack McCoy followed a strong moral compass and never rested unitl his duty to justice was fulfilled.






    Image Source: Wikipedia

  2. Detective Lenny Briscoe

    Jerry Orbach played stuborn but vigilant Detective Lennie Briscoe from seasons 4-14 until he left the show due to health issues. Arguably the face of the franchise, Lennie's snide remarks often took the form of memorable one-liners that defined the show. Although he had built a very sucessful career in Police work, Lennie was no stranger to trouble with the Law, as the devorcee's daughter was killed in a drug incident in season nine.






    Image Source: Wikipedia

  3. District Attorney Adam Schiff

    The longest tenured member of the original cast, Adam Schiff served ten years as the District Attorney for the City of New York. His well conected sphere of influence from years of serving as both an ADA and judge made him widely respected. A calm and careful thinker, Adam Schiff never rushed to conclusions and always considered his all of his options before making a decision. Frequently he had to restrain the bit-of-a-loose- cannon in Jack McCoy and he always served as a role model to McCoy. Responsible for maintaining fair and equal prosecution Schiff never wavered and had impecable judgement. After more than a decade as the District Attorney, Adam Schiff retired to help maintain the rights of those who needed them the most.



    Image Source: IMDb

  4. Capitan Anita Van Buren

    Beginning her tenure as the Capitan of the Precinct in Season 4, Anita van Buren led one of the most sucessful and well organized precincts in Manhattan. Known by many for her stern yet fair leadership, Van Buren recieved multiple awards for her work for the city and the NYPD. Often, when her detectives were unable to connect with a suspect, she stepped in without missing a beat. In addition to having a great relationship with her detectives and other members of the Precinct, Van Buren had a strong friendship with Jack McCoy and the District Attorneys office. In leadding a no nonsense opperation, she showed an incredible sense of dedication to her job and to her coworkers. In addition to her strong work ethic, her striking intellegence often shined through when she was needed the most.


    Image Source: TV Guide

  5. Detective Mike Logan

    An original member of the Law and Order Cast, Detective Mike Logan served for five years and with three different partners. In only two years, both his original parter and his replacment were injured on the job and were forced to retire to desk jobs within the Police force. Logan had a firey temper and often cared little for the comfort of suspects. Often stating his mind rather than swallowing his pride, Detective Logan was no strnager to conflict. Often not given the respect he deserved due to his age, he certainly wasn't shy to let his coworkers and superiors know how he really felt.
