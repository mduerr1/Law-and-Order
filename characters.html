Top 5 Characters with link to Actors' Wikipedias

  1. Assistant District Attorney Jack McCoy

    Beginning his Law and Order tenure in Season 5, ADA Jack McCoy prosecuted criminals for more than a decade. Folliwing his tenure as an ADA, Jack McCoy became the District Attorney, replacing his predecessor Arthur Branch. Known for his passion for his job and dedication to the law, Jack McCoy followed a strong moral compass and never rested until his duty to justice was fulfilled.
    Image Source: Wikipedia

  2. Detective Lennie Briscoe

    Jerry Orbach played stubborn but vigilant Detective Lennie Briscoe from seasons 4-14 until he left the show due to health issues. Arguably the face of the franchise, Lennie's snide remarks often took the form of memorable one-liners that defined the show.   3. District Attorney Adam Schiff

    The longest tenured member of the original cast, Adam Schiff served ten years as the District Attorney for the City of New York. His well connected sphere of influence from years of serving as an ADA made him widely respected. A calm and careful thinker, Adam Schiff never rushed to conclusions and always considered all of his options before making a decision. Frequently he had to restrain the loose cannon in Jack McCoy, whom he served as a role model. Responsible for maintaining fair and equal prosecution Schiff never wavered and had impeccable judgement. After more than a decade as the District Attorney, Adam Schiff retired to help maintain the rights of those who needed them the most.
    Image Source: IMDb

  4. Captain Anita Van Buren

    Beginning her tenure as the Precinct Captain in Season 4, Anita van Buren led one of the most successful and well organized precincts in Manhattan. Known by many for her stern yet fair leadership, Van Buren received multiple awards for her work for the city and the NYPD. Often, when her detectives were unable to connect with a suspect, she stepped in without missing a beat. In addition to having a great relationship with her detectives and other members of the Precinct, Van Buren had a strong friendship with Jack McCoy and the District Attorney's office. In leading a no nonsense operation, she showed an incredible sense of dedication to her job and coworkers. In addition to her strong work ethic, her striking intelligence often shined through when she was needed the most.
    Image Source: TV Guide

  5. Detective Mike Logan

    An original member of the Law and Order Cast, Detective Mike Logan served for five years with three different partners. In only two years, both his original partner and his replacement were injured on the job and were forced to retire to desk jobs. Logan had a firey temper and often cared little for the comfort of suspects. Often stating his mind rather than swallowing his pride, Detective Logan was no stranger to conflict. Frequently not given the respect he deserved due to his age, he certainly wasn't afraid to let his coworkers and superiors know how he really felt.
    Image Source: Wikipedia
